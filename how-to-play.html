How-to-Play

Layout

  • Images (center of the screen)
Gameplay
Selecting a Choice

Click one of the images at the center of the screen to select rock, paper, or scissors. If you guess correctly or incorrectly, or draw with the CPU, then the respective score on the scoreboard will increase by 1.

Postgame

If the player won or loss, a modal/pop-up window will appear containing text that indicates whether they won or lost, a retry button and a close button which takes them to the About page when clicked.
